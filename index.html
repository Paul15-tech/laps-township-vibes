Our Story

LAPS Entertainment Centre stands as a beacon of entrepreneurial excellence in Moletji, Limpopo. As a proudly black-owned township business, we represent the spirit of Ubuntu and community development that drives South African enterprise forward.

Our Vision

To be the premier entertainment and service destination in Limpopo, providing world-class facilities while celebrating African culture and hospitality.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional services across our diverse offerings - from professional car wash services to authentic shisanyama experiences, comfortable accommodation, and memorable events - all while contributing to the economic development of our community.
